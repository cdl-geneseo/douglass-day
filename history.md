# A Short History of Douglass Day

Jim Casey, Assistant Professor of English at the University of California Santa Barbara, founding director of Douglass Day, and co-founding director of the Center for Black Digital research, gives a quick introduction in the video below to the origin and subsequent history of a day dedicated to celebrating Frederick Douglass's birth, the achievements of Black Americans, and the issues and challenges that remain in the quest for a just and equitable society.
